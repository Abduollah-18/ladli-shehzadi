Meri Ladli Shehzadi ke Naam

Meri pyari ladli shehzadi, tum meri zindagi ki wo khushboo ho jo har subah mujhko jagati hai. Tumhari masarrat mere liye sabse badi dua hai. Har lafz jo yahan likha gaya hai, tumhari muskurahat ke liye likha gaya hai. Main chahta hoon ke humari zindagi me hamesha barkat aur mohabbat rahe. Allah se dua hai ke tum hamesha khush raho aur humari zindagi me rehmat barsti rahe.

Jab se tum meri zindagi me aayi ho, har pal ek naya rang le aaya. Tum meri dua ho, tum meri roshni ho. Tumhari awaz me ghar ka ehsaas hai aur tumhari muskurahat se din roshan ho jata hai. Ye lafz chhote ho sakte hain magar inka jazba gehra hai. Main chahata hoon ke har din tumhare liye ek nayi khushi lekar aaye.

Dreams

Hamare sapne sadhay magar dil se pyare hain. Main chahta hoon ke hum ek cozy ghar banayen jahan tumhara har khwab haqeeqat bane. Har saal hum ek nayi jagah ghoomenge, aur har raat choti choti baaton se apna dil bharenge. Dua karta hoon ke Allah hamari raah me asaniyaan paida kare.

Main sochta hoon ke agar hum sath duniya ghoom lein to bhi sabse zyada sukoon tumhari aankhon me milega. Har manzil par tumhara haath mera sahara hoga aur hamari kahani me nayi khushiyan likhi jayengi.
