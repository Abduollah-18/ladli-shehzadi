Humare Plans — Saath Saath

Chhoti chhoti khushiyoon se bharpur zindagi ke liye kuch dil se banaye hue iraade.

Humare Pyare Plans

Chalo kuch pyaare plans banate hain—aise jo calendar se zyaada dil par likhe jaayen. Pehla: har mahine ek chhoti date jahan hum sirf baatein karein, bina phone ke, bina waqt dekhe. Doosra: ek safar jahan hum bina map ke chalein aur shehar apne aap khul kar dikhaye. Teesra: ghar ke liye ek cozy kona, jahan tumhari pasand ki kitaabein, mera coffee mug, aur humari tasveerein ho. Chautha: har saal ek naya skill—kabhi cooking, kabhi painting—sirf isliye ke saath seekhna bhi pyaar hota hai. Aur sabse zaroori: har raat ek shukriya, ek ‘I love you’, ek ‘kal aur behtar hoga’.

Chhoti Khushiyan, Badi Baat

Main chahunga ke hum apni chhoti chhoti jeet ka jashn hamesha manayen—chahe wo ek sahi bana paratha ho ya kisi mushkil din ka theek se guzarna. Apne liye waqt nikaalna humara niyam hoga: Sunday morning ki lambi walk, shaam ki balcony wali chai, aur kabhi kabhi bina wajah ki late-night drives. Family ke saath muskurahat baantna, doston ko waqt dena, aur sabse badhkar ek dusre ko samajhna—yehi hamare plans ka dil hoga. Zindagi me perfect moment ka intezar nahi, har moment ko pyaara banana hamara style hoga.

Hamare Niyam

Hum decide karte hain ke hamara ghar ilzaam se nahi, ihsaan se bharega; takraar se nahi, samajh se chalega. Jab baatein kathin ho jayen, hum ruk kar sunenge; jab din lamba ho, hum gale mil kar chhota kar denge. Har hafte ek naya chhota goal, har mahine ek yaadgar pal—aur har saal, ek naya sapna jo milkar poora hoga.
