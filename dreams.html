Sapne — Tum, Main, Aur Kal

Woh sapne jo tumhari aankhon se zyada khoobsurat lagte hain jab hum saath dekhte hain.

Mere Sapne, Tumhari Chamk

Mere sapne bahut saade hain—magar tumhare saath unki chamak hazaar guna badh jati hai. Main ek aisa ghar dekhta hoon jahan subah tumhari hansi se shuru ho, aur raat tumhare ‘good night’ se mehfooz ho kar soti ho. Main chahata hoon hum duniya ke shehron ko dekhein—lekin sabse zyada, tumhari aankhon me chamak dekhun jab tum kisi naye nazare se milo. Ek din hum apni kahani ko likhenge bhi—shayed ek chhoti diary me, jismein ticket stubs, coffee stains, aur bahut saare hearts bane hon. Sapna bas itna sa hai: tum ho, main hoon, aur baaki sab khud se theek ho jaye.

Lamba Safar, Saath Saath

Aage ka raasta lambe safar jaisa lagta hai, aur mujhe ye lamba safar pasand hai kyun ke ismein tum ho. Main sapna dekhta hoon ek aise din ka jab hum apni balcony me baith kar purane gaane sun rahe hon, aur hawa me tumhari mehek ghuli ho. Hum apni chhotiyaan jeeton ka collection banayenge—tasveerein, chitthiyan, kabhi kabhi ek dusre ke liye chhoti surprises. Zindagi jab kabhi mushkil ho, hum dono ek team ki tarah khade hon—‘it’s us versus the problem’. Aur jab aasman me taare hon, main bas ek dua maangoonga: meri Ladli Shehzadi hamesha muskurati rahe.

Bas Itna Sa Khwab

Aakhir me bas yahi chahta hoon ke jab tum peeche mud kar dekho to har jagah apne pairon ke nishan ke saath meri muskurahat bhi mile. Hamari zindagi ek geet ho, jismein sur kabhi dheeme, kabhi tez—lekin hamesha meetha. Aur jab bhi zindagi kuch zyada ho jaye, hum dono apni favorite chai bana kar balcony me baith jayein, aur yaad karein ke pyaar hi hamara pehla, beech ka, aur aakhri raasta hai.
